Under the sea

Welcome! In this beautiful adventure to explore the depths of the sea.

Explore the seabed collecting the keys and find the mighty treasure lost in here centuries ago.

How to play:

Move the submarine using the buttons of the control panel or the ones on your keyboard. Your goal is to collect all the keys by touching them, but be aware and avoid the sharks. Once you'll have find the treasure casket click on it to win.

Commands:

  • W - move foward
  • Q - dive
  • E - emerge
  • A - turn left
  • D - turn right
  • S - move backward
